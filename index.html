Automate your world

ECE Pulse is an annual student-run conference held at Illinois that showcases innovations in the field of electrical and computer engineering. The conference is comprised of design competitions, hands-on workshops, tech talks, and networking sessions for students, faculty, alumni, and corporate representatives. Our aim is to inspire students in their academic pursuits by connecting them with cutting-edge technology and innovative projects in the corporate world. This year's theme — Automate your World — will focus on the ways our world is evolving to become more connected and collaborative every day.
